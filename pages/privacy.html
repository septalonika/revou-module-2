Privacy and Policy

  1. Introduction

    Introduction Welcome to Hotelolet Jakarta, the number one hotel in Jakarta. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with applicable laws, including the Indonesian Personal Data Protection Law.

  2. Information We Collect

    We may collect personal information from you when you:

    • Make a reservation or inquiry
    • Check-in or check-out
    • Use our services and facilities
    • Participate in surveys or promotions

    The types of personal data we may collect include:

    • Name
    • Contact information (email address, phone number)
    • Payment information
    • Preferences and feedback
    • Any other information you provide to us

  3. How We Use Your Information

    Your personal data may be used for the following purposes:

    • To process your reservations and transactions
    • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries
    • To personalize your experience at our hotel
    • To send promotional materials and updates about our services
    • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

  4. Sharing Your Information

    We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information with:

    • Service providers who assist us in operating our hotel (e.g., payment processors, marketing partners)
    • Authorities when required by law or to protect our rights

    All third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

  5. Data Security

    We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

  6. Your Rights

    You have the right to:

    • Access your personal data held by us
    • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
    • Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances
    • Withdraw consent for processing your personal data

    To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

  7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
